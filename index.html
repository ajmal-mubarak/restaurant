"Cooking is not just food — it is emotion, culture and love on a plate."

Chef Roberto Carlos

Executive Head Chef

With over two decades in the culinary world, Chef Roberto has trained across kitchens in Mumbai, Dubai and London. He returned to Kerala to celebrate its rich food heritage — combining traditional Malabar recipes with modern fine-dining techniques. His philosophy is simple: use the freshest local ingredients and cook with intention.

Under his leadership, Delicious Bites has won multiple regional awards and become the go-to dining destination in Kannur.
